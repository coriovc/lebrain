About us

Working continuously since 1972 in the development of engineering projects and consultancy specialized in agribusiness and its associated processes.

Created to offer consultancy in the agricultural market, IEP is the gateway to Agri–business. IEP has the know-how, the structure and experience to ensure management professionalism and transparency. Moreover,in the last fifteen years, IEP has specialized in the production of commodities for agroindustry. And has developed sunflower oil production with high content of oleic acid, among other services to leading companies.

IEP combines a multi-disciplinary approach with deep, practical industry and agricultural knowledge to help clients meet challenges and respond to opportunities.
