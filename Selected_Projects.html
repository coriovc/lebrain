Selected Projects

IEP develops highly specialized studies and projects from the economic-financial analysis and engineering up to their startup and commissioning.

IEP’s Technicians have developed projects for different public and private organizations, through services of organization, management, advice and restructuring of companies.

Management & Consulting Services
  • Ongoing projects
  • Irrigation, Equipment, and Infrastructure Construction: Quizenga, Malanje, Angola. Construction of a dam and planting of 5,000ha of agriculture. U$D 122Mi total Capex.
  • Farming in Argentina: Provinces of Córdoba, Buenos Aires and Chaco. 10,000ha of own production of maize, soybeans, sunflowers, wheat and cotton.
  • Fazenda Vissati Farming and Agroindustry complex: Cuando Cubango, Angola. 9,200ha project design, execution, and management. Corn, soybeans and beans under irrigation. Family farming assistance.
  • Completed Projects
  • Agricultural Funds Management in Argentina (FAID) with International Financial Corporation (IFC) as anchor investor (1994 - 2014): U$D 205Mi invested in over 1Mi ha with 2.5Mi+ Tn of production.

  • Polo Agroindustrial Quizenga (Malanje) & Polo Agroindustrial Cubal(Benguela), Angola: 60.000ha under management. Design and execution of a 18.000Tn grain storage facility and maize flour, soya and bean processing productions facilities. U$D 94Mi of total Capex deployed.
Agricultural Development
