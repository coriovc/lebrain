iHunt

Candidate selection system

Chile

HR Tech / Chile

  • Rebranding
  • Social Media Strategy
  • Content creation
  • FB/IG Ad Campaigns assets

iHunt is a Chilean startup with reach to the entire region through its system that combines artificial intelligence and a network of independent recruiters, allowing clients to obtain a shortlist of candidates in half the time and with half the budget.

Our challenge was to identify the positioning of the company and from there develop a complete identity that represents the full potential of iHunt.

With the identity already developed and underway, we are in the midst of developing its content and social media strategy.

Since the company is currently adjusting the business model, we will soon develop a new website and digital elements.
