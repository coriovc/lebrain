What we do

At IEP we have a technical team working for 45 years continuously in the development of engineering projects and consultancy specialized in agribusiness and its associated processes.

We have developed our activities in Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, Guatemala, Ukraine, Chile, Uruguay, the Philippines, Malaysia, and Angola.
